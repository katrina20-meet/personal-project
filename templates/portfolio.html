Identify Danger!

Beware! fur on thy face

Susie the Strangler, notorious serial killer, ended many lives by tricking poeple into letting her sleep in the bed....
then choking them by sleeping on their face. She would often test her balance on the face before the murder. Your cat may not be as professional as Susie,
but the danger of a furry death may hang above your head should your cat enter your bed.

Do I smell smoke?

With their tiny adorable paws, and small furry body, cats seem like the last creature to be capable of starting a fire.
But Dante, more known as The Inferno, proved that wrong. With fast, nearly untraceable movements, Dante would set dog shelters on fire, after stealing their food.
He danced in circles around the police for years before they could catch him.
should your house begin to smell of smoke, beware!

Shadow behind the shower curtain

Cats are commonly known to have a fear of water.
A ripple and splash would be sure to chase them away. But Mer, an underrated criminal virtuoso,
also known as The Cat That Walks On Water, was untroubled by it, as it was her ultimate weapon.
sneaking up on poeple as they bathed, she would dunk their head below the surface and hold it there until
they lost their breath. Fear any cat that shows an interest in water!
